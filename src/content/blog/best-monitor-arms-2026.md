## Why Use a Monitor Arm?

- **Ergonomic** — Position screen at eye level
- **Desk space** — Free up room for other stuff
- **Flexibility** — Move monitor wherever you want
- **Multiple monitors** — Some arms hold 2+

## How to Choose

Consider:

1. **Monitor size** — Make sure arm supports your size
2. **Weight capacity** — Check your monitor's weight
3. **Mount type** — Clamp vs grommet vs wall
4. **Features** — Do you need tilt, swivel, rotate?

---

## Installation Tips

- **Clamp mount** — Needs desk edge under 2"
- **Grommet mount** — Needs hole in desk
- **Position height** — Top of monitor at eye level
- **Distance** — Arm's length away from eyes
