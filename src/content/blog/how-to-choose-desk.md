Buying a desk seems simple. But with so many options, it's easy to get overwhelmed.

## Question 1: How Much Space Do You Have?

- **Small room:** Consider a compact desk or wall-mounted
- **Medium room:** Standard 48-60" desk works
- **Large room:** L-shaped or expansive desk possible

## Question 2: Sitting or Standing?

### Sit-Stand Desks
- Switch between sitting and standing
- More expensive ($200+)
- Benefits: Better for long-term health

### Traditional Sitting Desks
- Cheaper ($100-300)
- More stable
- Choose if you only sit

## Question 3: What's Your Budget?

| Budget | Best Option |
|--------|-------------|
| Under $100 | Basic desk, no features |
| $100-200 | Budget standing or solid sitting |
| $200-400 | Good standing desk |
| $400+ | Premium everything |
