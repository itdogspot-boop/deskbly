Choosing the right desk is the foundation of a productive home office.

## Consider Your Space

Measure your room first. Know exactly how much floor space you have.

## Desk Types

### Standing Desks
Best for: Those who want to alternate between sitting and standing.
- Electric height adjustable
- Manual crank options
- Best brands: FlexiSpot, UPLIFT

### L-Shaped Desks
Best for: Corner spaces and dual monitor setups.
- Maximizes corner space
- Room for multiple monitors

### Regular Desks
Best for: Simple setups and tight budgets.
- Affordable
- Many size options

## Key Features to Look For

1. **Height Adjustability** - Game changer for comfort
2. **Weight Capacity** - Ensure it holds your monitors
3. **Cable Management** - Keep wires organized
4. **Durability** - Solid build quality

| Feature | Why It Matters |
|---------|---------------|
| Height Adjustable | Ergonomic comfort |
| Cable Management | Clean setup |
| Wide Surface | Multiple monitors |
| Stable Base | No wobbling |
