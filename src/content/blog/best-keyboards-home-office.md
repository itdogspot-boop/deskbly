## Switch Types Explained

If buying mechanical, you'll need to choose switches:

- **Red** — Linear, smooth, quiet (gaming)
- **Brown** — Tactile, moderate (typing + gaming)
- **Blue** — Clicky, loud (typing)

For work: **Brown switches.** Tactile feedback without annoying your coworkers.
