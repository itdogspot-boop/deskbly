## What to Look For Under $200

At this price, you're making trade-offs. Here's what matters most:

- **Adjustable lumbar** — Non-negotiable. Your lower back needs support
- **Adjustable armrests** — Nice to have, but don't sacrifice lumbar for arms
- **Mesh vs. padding** — Mesh breathes; padding is softer
- **Weight capacity** — Make sure it can hold you
