Back pain is one of the most common issues for remote workers. The wrong chair makes it worse. The right chair can actually help.

Here's our guide to the best chairs for back pain.

## What Causes Back Pain While Sitting?

- **Poor lumbar support** — Lower back needs support
- **Wrong seat height** — Feet should be flat, knees at 90°
- **No adjustment** — One size doesn't fit all
- **Bad posture** — Slouching puts pressure on spine

The fix: An ergonomic chair with adjustable lumbar support.

## Tips for Back Pain Relief

Even with the best chair:

1. **Take breaks** — Stand up every 30-60 minutes
2. **Adjust your chair** — Lumbar should support your lower back
3. **Monitor height** — Top of monitor at eye level
4. **Stretch** — Simple stretches during breaks help
