## Why Your Chair Matters More Than Your Desk

Here's the thing: you can have the most expensive standing desk in the world, but if you're sitting in a terrible chair, you're going to feel it.

Most people spend 6-10 hours a day sitting. That's half your waking life. A good ergonomic chair:

- Supports your lumbar spine (lower back)
- Keeps your knees at 90 degrees
- Allows your feet to rest flat
- Reduces hip pressure
- Prevents slouching

The wrong chair? It causes back pain, hip pain, neck strain, and poor circulation. It's not worth saving $100.

## Don't Buy These

A quick note on what to avoid — these are popular but not worth your money:

### "Gaming Chairs"
90% are overpriced ($200-500) and poorly designed for actual sitting. Racing bucket seats are designed for cars, not desks. They look cool but destroy your back over time.

### Amazon Chairs Under $100
They fall apart in 6 months. The "ergonomic" claims are marketing, not reality. Your back is worth more than $100.

### Anything Without Adjustable Lumbar
If you can't adjust the lower back support, it's not an ergonomic chair. It's a stool with delusions.
