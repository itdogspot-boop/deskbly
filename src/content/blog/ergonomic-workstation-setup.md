Set up your workstation properly to avoid back pain and stay productive.

## Chair Setup

Your chair is the most important piece.

- Feet flat on floor
- Knees at 90 degrees
- Back fully supported
- Armrests at desk height

## Monitor Position

- Top of screen at eye level
- Arm's length away
- Slight tilt upward

## Keyboard and Mouse

- Elbows at 90 degrees
- wrists straight, not bent
- Mouse close to keyboard

## Lighting

- Reduce glare on screen
- Task lighting for documents
- Natural light preferred

| Element | Position |
|---------|----------|
| Monitor | Arm's length |
| Chair Height | Feet flat |
| Desk Height | Elbows at 90° |
