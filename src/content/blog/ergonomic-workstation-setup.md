You bought the perfect chair and desk. But if your setup isn't configured right, you're still risking injury.

## The Golden Rules

1. **Eyes level with top of monitor**
2. **Elbows at 90° when typing**
3. **Feet flat on floor**
4. **Wrists straight, not bent**
5. **20-26" from eyes to screen**

## Step 1: Chair Height

- Sit with feet flat on floor
- Adjust seat height so thighs are parallel
- Ensure 2-3 fingers fit between seat edge and knees
- Adjust armrests so elbows are at 90°

## Step 2: Monitor Position

- Top of monitor at eye level
- Monitor 20-26" from eyes (arm's length)
- Monitor centered in front of you
- Tilted slightly back (10-20°)

## Step 3: Keyboard and Mouse

- Keyboard centered in front of you
- Mouse right next to keyboard
- Wrasts straight, not bent up or down
