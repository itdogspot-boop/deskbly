If you have a corner in your home office, you're sitting on prime real estate. An L-shaped desk uses that corner space efficiently, giving you room for dual monitors, paperwork, and still have space for a coffee cup or plant.

We researched 25+ L-shaped desks to find the ones that actually work. Here's what made the cut.

## Why Choose an L-Shaped Desk?

Before we get to the list, here's why you might want an L-desk:

- **Maximize corner space** — Use dead space that most desks ignore
- **Dual monitor setup** — Room for 2 monitors without crowding
- **Work and personal** — Separate work and personal setups on different sides
- **More surface area** — 50-80% more desk space than a standard desk

The trade-off: L-desks are bigger. Make sure you have the room.

## Tips for L-Desk Placement

1. **Corner is best** — Use that dead space
2. **Mind the traffic flow** — Don't block doorways
3. **Measure twice** — Make sure it'll fit through your doors
4. **Consider your monitor** — You'll want the long side for monitors
