Gaming chairs look cool. But can you actually work in one?

## Gaming Chairs: The Good

- Look cool
- Recline function
- Padded armrests
- Cushioned seat

## Gaming Chairs: The Bad

- Bucket seat design restricts movement
- Vinyl/leather gets hot
- Not designed for work
- Less ergonomic support
- Worn out faster (2-3 years)

## Office Chairs: The Good

- Ergonomic design for 8+ hours
- Mesh options breathe
- Adjustable lumbar
- Lasts 5-10 years

## Our Recommendation

For work: **Office chair.** Every time.

The ergonomic support alone makes office chairs worth it.
