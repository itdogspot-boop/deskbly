Not everyone can spend $300+ on a standing desk. But here's the good news: you don't have to.

We've found standing desks under $200 that actually work. They're not perfect, but they'll get the job done without breaking the bank.

## What to Expect Under $200

At this price point, you're making trade-offs:

- **Single motors** — Slower than dual motor desks
- **Smaller desktops** — Usually 40-48"
- **Fewer features** — No memory presets on some models
- **Basic build quality** — Still functional, just not premium

But they work. And for many people, that's enough.
