Living in a small apartment or bedroom doesn't mean you can't have a productive workspace. The trick is choosing a desk designed for small spaces — not just a "small desk."

We tested 20+ compact desks to find the ones that actually work. Here's what fits.

## What Makes a Desk "Small Space Ready"?

Before buying, check these:

- **Actual dimensions** — Measure your space. Compare to the desk, not just "compact" marketing
- **Weight** — Can you move it yourself?
- **Storage** — Some compact desks include storage to make up for lost surface area
- **Multi-function** — Some fold, some stack, some transform

## Pro Tips for Small Spaces

1. **Measure twice** — Know your exact dimensions before buying
2. **Go vertical** — Wall-mounted options save floor space
3. **Consider the chair** — Make sure you have room to sit
4. **Think about storage** — Compact desks often lack storage; plan accordingly
